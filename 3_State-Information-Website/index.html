Ohio

Ohio is a state located in the Midwest region of the United States. [Wikipedia - Ohio] It is known for its diverse geography, which includes farmland, forests, and large cities. Ohio is the 17th most populous state in the U.S. and is often recognized for its key role in American history, industry, and politics. In this website you will find all you need to know about Cleveland, Dayton, and the states capital Columbus.

3 Random Facts About Ohio

  1. Ohio is home to the first modern-day professional baseball team, the Cincinnati Red Stockings.
  2. It is a major industrial hub, particularly in the automotive and steel industries, and was a key player in America's industrial revolution.
  3. Ohio is known as the birthplace of flight, as it was home to the Wright brothers, who made the first successful flight. [50states - Ohio]

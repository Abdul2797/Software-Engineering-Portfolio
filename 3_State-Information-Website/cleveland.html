Cleveland

Cleveland, Ohio, is a major city in the northern part of the state, located along the southern shore of Lake Erie. With a population of approximately 362,000, it is one of Ohio's largest cities and serves as an important cultural and economic hub. [Wikipedia - Cleveland - Ohio] Cleveland is known for its strong healthcare industry, vibrant arts and music scene, and rich sports culture. The city is home to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, renowned museums, and major sports teams, making it a key destination for entertainment and tourism.

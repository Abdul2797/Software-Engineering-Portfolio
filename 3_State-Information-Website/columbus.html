Columbus

Columbus, Ohio, is the state capital and largest city in Ohio, with a population of approximately 905,000. [Wikipedia - Columbus - Ohio] It is located in the central part of the state, which gives it strategic importance in both economic and transportation networks. Columbus is known for its diverse economy, strong educational institutions, and thriving tech industry.
